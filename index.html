What is SCA?

Scrollback Campus Ambassadors are leaders, who are enthusiastic community builders.



Who are We?


Scrollback Campus Ambassadors are leaders, who are enthusiastic community builders, leading campaigns at their colleges and within their communities to encourage others to use Scrollback as well as contribute to this Open Source project.



What we Do?


We Create (and maintain) Scrollback rooms for our respective colleges (or courses), Promote Scrollback within our colleges and communities and encourage others to create their own online communities using Scrollback.



How do we do it?


The Campus Ambassadors are mentored by the core team members of the Scrollback community to use Scrollback and also help others to use Scrollback and Ambassadors have online meetings every alternate week, to give an update on the status of their work.

Who, How, What?

Who can be a part of SCA, How can you be a part of SCA, What benefits for you.

WHO CAN BE SCA?


  • If You are a student
  • If You are an Open Source enthusiast
  • If You are a community builder (or willing to be one)

HOW CAN YOU BE SCA?


  • Join Us at Scrollback Events
  • Join Us at Scrollback Campus Ambassadors Room
  • Apply For Scrollback buddy through the form here

WHAT BENEFITS FOR YOU AS SCA?


  • Opportunity to be 'Ambassadors of the Month'
  • Spread the web and gain marketing experience
  • SCA(s) will be awarded by a letter of recommendation
